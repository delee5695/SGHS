Dormitory

Does everyone live in the dormitory?

Occasionally, students commute to school, but most students live in dormitories regardless of where they live.

How many people use the dormitory?

Most of the rooms are double occupancy, but first-year female students sometimes use a four-person occupancy room during one semester.

How do you allocate the roommate and room?

Room allocation is conducted every semester, completely randomly and through lottery.

School

Do you really teach classes in English?

Basically, English is used for all subjects except Korean and Korean history. Online class video or class English is also used a lot for materials (PPT, etc.).

What subjects do you study other than Korean, English, and math?

Select and learn Chinese, French, or Spanish as a second foreign language, and AP subjects as AP In addition to microeconomics, macroeconomics, humanities and geography, and modern world history, social studies subjects include international politics.

Are there any programs that support overseas college admissions?

For students who want to go to an overseas university, special lectures by seniors and teachers, classes for tests such as AP, SAT, There are consultations with teachers of the department of overseas university entrance.

Activities

Isn't it difficult to study basic subjects after doing various activities?

Basic subjects represented by Korean, English, and mathematics are conducted not only during basic class hours, but also after school, summer vacation classes, and individual self-study time. also supports learning

What is your daily routine like?

I wake up at 6:50 in the morning, exercise, have breakfast, and go to school by 8 am. school from 8:00 to 4:00 After class, we have dinner at 5pm and self-study from 6:20 to 10:50. After the tutorial is over Return to the dormitory and sleep freely. There is no set bedtime or turn off time.
