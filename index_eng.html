Our Hearts in Korea, Our Eyes to the World

Seoul International High School is a public international high school that opened on May 7, 2008 and is located at 40 Sungkyunkwan-ro 13-gil, Jongno-gu, the center of Seoul. The educational goal is to cultivate international professionals with both intellectual virtues, and we have adopted plum blossoms as a school flower, pine trees as a school tree, and taekwondo as a school tree.

This website is produced in cooperation with the school's stdent council, focusing on the activities of the students of this school ,and is operated with the sponsorship of Jongno-gu Office, which hopes to provide education suitable for the 4th revolution. The contents registered here are loaded with the confirmation of the teacher in charge, and the official website address of Seoul International High School operated by the Seoul Metropolitan Office of Education is https://sghs.sen.hs.kr

In the case of school classes, classes in various subjects are provided according to the educational goal of fostering international professionals with both knowledge and virtue. Subjects other than school-designated subjects such as English, Mathematics, and AP Microeconomics are subject to students' aptitude You can choose and take various subjects such as AP calculus, AP macroeconomics, international politics, and Korean language in the era of internationalization. Also, there are French, Spanish, and Chinese as second languages. As part of bilingual education, in all subjects except for some, Classes are conducted using English, and Korean teachers and native teachers are co-teaching.
